Calendar of Events
2014–2015

Speaker Series presentations take place on the second Thursday of the month unless otherwise noted.

All presentations will be held at the Dundas Museum & Archives, 139 Park St. West (at Albert). Doors open at 7 p.m. and the presentations begin at 7:30. There is a $3 admission fee ($2 for members). Seating is limited.

September 2014
11 Dr. Martin Horn Once More Into the Breach: Causes of the Great War Dr. Horn is Associate Professor of History, McMaster University, and has authored several books on the Great War, focusing on the financing of the war and the politics of industrial collaboration.

October 2014
16 Dr. Beryl Haslam We Remember: Occupied Holland, The London Blitz, and the Lady Intelligence Officer Note: This presentation takes place on the 3rd Thursday of the month.
Dr. Beryl Haslam, Professor of History (retired), Moderator. Panel Members: Chris Kruter, Clive Freeman and Keith Green.

November 2014
13 Cameron Ward The 77th Regiment: A Brief History Cameron Ward, BA, History and Politics, McMaster University. Military Historian, Collector and Curator
