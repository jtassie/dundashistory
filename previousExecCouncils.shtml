Executive Council 2013–2014

At the Annual General Meeting which took place in May, 2013, the membership of the Dundas Valley Historical Society elected the following individuals to serve on the Executive Council for 2013–2014:

Mike Brand (Executive Member At Large, A-V Coordinator)
